Pick a Location

Location is Key

Here are three easy tips for choosing the perfect location for your new garden.

  1. Most fruits and vegetables need 6 to 8 hours of sunlight daily. Observe your yard throughout the day to find the sunniest spot. Avoid areas heavily shaded by trees, buildings, or fences.
  2. Choose a spot with well-draining soil. No standing pools of water after a rain. Make sure it is not too sandy or clay-heavy. You might need to improve it with compost.
  3. Make sure your garden is near a convenient water source. Lugging watering cans across the yard can get old fast! Having easy access to a hose or irrigation system will make maintenance easier.
